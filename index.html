Foundations of RoboticsÂ¶

The goal of this project is to provide a comprehensive introduction and overview to the foundations of robotics. I will mainly focus on the theoretical foundations of robotics, including kinematics, dynamics, control, motion planning, perception, and nowdays, machine learning, deep learning, reinforcement learning, and other modern techniques. I stand on the shoulders of giants, and I will try to provide a comprehensive overview of the field, but only focusing on the most important concepts and theory.
